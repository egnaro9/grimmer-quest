# Privacy Policy for Glimmer Quest

**Last Updated: March 31, 2026**

## Introduction
Glimmer Quest ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Player Name**: The display name you choose when starting the game
- **Game Progress**: Your scores, levels, achievements, and in-game purchases

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: Game sessions, features used, crash reports
- **Advertising ID**: Used for showing relevant ads (can be reset in device settings)

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the game
- To save your progress and sync across devices
- To show personalized ads
- To process in-app purchases
- To improve our game and fix bugs
- To display leaderboards

## Third-Party Services

### Advertising
We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Payments
We use Stripe for in-app purchases. Payment information is processed securely by Stripe and is not stored on our servers. See [Stripe's Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy).

### Analytics
We may use analytics services to understand how players use our game. This data is anonymized and aggregated.

## Data Retention
